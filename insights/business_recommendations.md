# Business Recommendations

## Operational Bottlenecks
Document Review demonstrated the highest operational backlog severity, with the largest concentration of applications delayed over 10 days.

Recommendation:
- implement escalation monitoring
- improve document collection workflows
- automate missing documentation reminders

---

## Regional Performance
The Northeast region achieved the strongest overall funding performance with minimal operational delays.

Recommendation:
- benchmark Northeast operational processes
- replicate approval workflows across lower-performing regions

---

## Customer Risk
Lower credit score bands demonstrated substantially higher late-payment risk.

Recommendation:
- strengthen underwriting controls
- increase monitoring of high-risk portfolios
- improve customer risk segmentation

---

## Retention Trends
Certain acquisition channels demonstrated elevated churn and delinquency behavior.

Recommendation:
- optimize acquisition targeting
- evaluate onboarding quality
- improve retention outreach strategies
